A1 Polymer (officially A-One Polymer Limited) is a prominent Bangladeshi manufacturer of plastic building materials and a key subsidiary of the Anwar Group of Industries. Established in 2006, it has grown to become one of the largest manufacturers of uPVC pipes and fittings in the sub-continent.

Core Business Operations
Specialization: Leading manufacturer of uPVC pipes, fittings, and bathroom solutions.
Production Capacity: Monthly capacity of approximately 450 MT for uPVC products and 20 MT for Teflon tape.
Technology: Utilizes advanced German technology and state-of-the-art inspection equipment to ensure product durability.
Market Reach: Supplies products across Bangladesh and exports to international markets including India, Bhutan, and Singapore.

Key Products
The company offers a wide range of plumbing and bathroom products: 
Pipes & Fittings: uPVC, cPVC, SWR (Soil, Waste, and Rain), and HDPE pipes.
Bathroom Fittings: ABS bathroom fittings, magic pipes, toilet tissue holders, bib cocks, and moving showers.
Industrial Solutions: Teflon (PTFE) thread seal tape, centrifugal pumps, and irrigation pumps.
